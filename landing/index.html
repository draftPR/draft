Stop babysitting your AI agents

Alma is the autonomous delivery pipeline between "describe what you want" and "merge to main." It generates tickets from your codebase, plans the work, runs agents in isolated branches, verifies with tests, and delivers reviewed diffs. You just review and ship.

From goal to merged PR

Point Alma at your repo. Describe what you want. Come back to review diffs.

1 Describe a goal

Tell Alma what you want built. It reads your codebase and generates scoped, accurate tickets with dependency ordering. No hallucinated file paths.

2 Autonomous planning

The planner builds a dependency DAG, queues tickets in order, and dispatches AI agents. It auto-follows-up on failures and proposes fixes.

3 Isolated execution + verification

Each ticket runs in its own git worktree. After the agent finishes, verification commands (tests, lints) run automatically. Failures get retried.

4 Review, merge, ship

Full diff viewer with inline comments. Approve, request changes, or let agents iterate. One click to merge or auto-create a GitHub PR.
